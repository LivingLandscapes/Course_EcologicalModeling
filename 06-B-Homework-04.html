Homework 4

Overview

In this homework assignment, you will analyze a dataset on Forest Bird densities from 56 patches in south eastern Victoria, in Australia. The aim of the study was to relate bird density to 6 habitat covariates: patch area (AREA), distance to nearest patch (DIST), distance to nearest larger patch (LDIST), altitude (ALT), the year the patch was isolated by vegetation clearing (YEAR.ISOL), and a categorical index of stock grazing intensity (GRAZE; 1 = light, 5 = heavy). The data are in the file ‘Loyn.txt’. Conduct a full analysis of these data, including data exploration. Be sure to identify non-linear relationships as appropriate, and think carefully about what the appropriate error distribution is for these data. Submit a .Rmd and .html file containing a methods section describing your analytical process, and a results section describing the results.

Recall from island biogeography theory that species richness should increase with AREA and decrease with isolation (DIST, LDIST, and YR.ISOL). Grazing is expected to have a negative effect on forest bird abundance because of the effects on understory vegetation. All species are lumped together in this response.

NOTE: You don’t need to use an HGAM here, but you can if you feel inclined.
